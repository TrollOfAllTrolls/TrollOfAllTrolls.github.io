Favorite Hobby

To be fair, I do many things to pass the time, such as participating in camps and goofing around with friends. However, the one thing that I can say I do most is game on my computing device. I play a variety of games to pass the time when I have nothing else to do, but my two favorite games that I can think of have to be Garry's Mod and Planetside 2. Garry's Mod is a modification of another game (called Half Life 2) that turns the game into a sandbox of sorts, where you can construct and do virtullary anything, as long as it's within the engine's limits Planetside 2 is a Massively Multiplayer Online FPS (MMOFPS) that takes place in the far future, where you are allowed to take part in a massive scale conflict by joining one of the three playable factions. The game centers around coordination and cooperation through smaller squads of players in order to complete objectives.
